Who We Are

Company Profile

Born from a passion for seamless design and meaningful technology, we set out to reinvent an everyday object — the mirror — into a portal of intelligent, ambient information.

Our team of engineers, designers, and product thinkers believes that the best technology disappears into your life, serving you quietly and beautifully.

Our strategy

How We Reach You

Current Marketing Strategy

We lead with story, not specification. Our campaigns live at the intersection of lifestyle and technology — placing the Smart Mirror inside the spaces people love most: homes, studios, boutique hotels.

Precision digital targeting, design-press partnerships, and flagship retail experiences form the core of how we build awareness and desire.

See our objectives

Where We're Headed

Objectives

Our near-term focus: launch in three flagship markets, establish category leadership, and build a subscription ecosystem around daily utility — weather, calendar, health, home control.

Long-term, we aim to make the Smart Mirror as ubiquitous as the smartphone, and as personal as the spaces it inhabits.

Our target market

Who We Serve

Target Market

Design-forward homeowners aged 28–50 who already live within the Apple ecosystem. They value aesthetics as much as utility and are willing to invest in objects that elevate their environment.

A secondary audience includes boutique hospitality, luxury fitness studios, and premium co-living spaces — venues where first impressions are everything.

Our market strategy

The Path Forward

Market Strategy

Premium positioning, ecosystem lock-in, and experiential retail. We don't compete on price — we compete on desire. Every touchpoint, from unboxing to daily use, is crafted to feel inevitable.

Strategic retail placement in Apple Stores, architecture showrooms, and design fairs creates the aspiration. Direct-to-consumer channels close the sale on our terms.
