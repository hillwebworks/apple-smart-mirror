Apple Inc. was established in 1976 in Cupertino, California. The founders' goal was to develop and sell powerful personal computers and make them accessible to ordinary people. This vision has grown into a worldwide technology enterprise. Apple now sits in the top 3 most valuable companies in the world by market capitalization, valued at over $4 trillion. Apple reported revenues of $416.16 billion in the financial year 2025 and profits of $112 billion — the record profit in the company's history. The company has over 150,000 employees worldwide.

The iPhone is the most popular Apple product. In 2025, Apple sold around 247 million phones worldwide, and over 1.5 billion iPhones exist currently. Other products include the Apple Watch, which tracks health and fitness, iPad, Mac, and AirPods. All these products are priced at a premium — a good example is the iPhone 17 Pro Max, which goes for over $1,500.

Apple has a mission to deliver the best customer experience. All of its products operate within the same ecosystem. The iPhone is at the center of this ecosystem. Through the iPhone, users can connect their Apple Watches, AirPods, and Mac, enabling seamless sharing of data. The company has very high customer retention. The iPhone in particular has a customer retention rate of 92% — the highest in the smartphone industry (SQ Magazine, 2025). This ecosystem is critical because Apple can cross-sell new products to its existing customer base.

Apple Smart Mirror will belong in the Wearables, Home, and Accessories segment — the same segment as Apple Watch and AirPods. The Smart Mirror will extend the value of these products. Apple Watch already tracks health metrics like sleep duration; the Apple Smart Mirror will surface that data. During a morning routine, users can see personalized summaries of their health metrics, daily goals such as workout progress, their personal calendar, and the weather forecast.

Currently, no Apple product serves this purpose — a gap that the Smart Mirror will fill. The Smart Mirror also deepens consumer investment in the Apple ecosystem, which will increase customer loyalty. Research shows that users who own multiple Apple products have an 87% lower rate of switching brands than those with a single device (TechLila, 2025). Apple Smart Mirror will reinforce this behavior and push that number even higher.
